Understanding Solunar Theory

Major Periods (≈2h) occur when the moon is directly overhead or underfoot — highest gravitational pull, strongest fish feeding response.


Minor Periods (≈1h) occur during moonrise and moonset — moderate activity increase.


New & Full Moons produce the strongest solunar effects. Quarter moons are weakest.


Best times are when a major period coincides with dawn or dusk.
